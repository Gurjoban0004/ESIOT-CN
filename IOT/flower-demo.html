Bottom-Corner Botanical Accents

This prototype shows hand-drawn wild rose and sage-berry SVG arrangements fixed exclusively to the bottom-left and bottom-right viewport corners.

The colors are pulled directly from the Prep.io palette — warm terracotta petals, dusty sage leaves, golden stamen centers — so they feel native to the existing design rather than pasted on.

Design Principles

Bottom corners only. No top decorations, no side elements, no floating petals. Just two carefully composed botanical illustrations anchored to the bottom edge.

Zero obstruction. The SVGs are layered behind all content with pointer-events: none and z-index: 0.

Barely-there sway. A ~0.8 degree rotation cycle that feels like a gentle breeze, not a carnival ride.

What You See

Bottom-left: A wild rose branch with a full multi-petal bloom, a half-open bud, veined leaves at natural angles, and a tiny closed bud on a twig.

Bottom-right: A sage-berry branch with rounded leaf clusters, a soft bloom, a berry cluster with highlights, and small buds on a twig.

Toggle dark mode to see how the botanicals shift to deeper forest tones.
